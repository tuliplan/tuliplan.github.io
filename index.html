Hailan Ma was born in Xiangyang, China, in 1992.
She received the B.E. degree in Automation and the M.Sc. degree in Control Science and Engineering from
Nanjing University, Nanjing, China, in 2014 and 2017, respectively.
Her research interests include machine learning and quantum control.
Currently, she is a research assisstant in Department of Control and System Engineering in Nanjing University. She will pursue her PhD degree in University of New South Wales.
