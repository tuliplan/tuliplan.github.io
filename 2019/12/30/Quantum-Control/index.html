Introduction to Quantum Control

Hailan Ma
2019-12-30
introduction
learning

Quantum control theory is a rapidly evolving research area, which has developed over
the last three decades. Controlling quantum phenomena has been an implicit
goal of much quantum physics and chemistry research since the establishment of quantum
mechanics.

To have a better understanding of quantum control, please refer to the following resources.

  1. M. A. Nielsen and I. L. Chuang (2010), Quantum Computation and Quantum Information, Cambridge University Press.
  2. H. M. Wiseman and G. J. Miburn (2010), Quantum Measurement and Control, Cambridge University Press.
  3. J. Biamonte, P. Wittek, N. Pancotti, P. Rebentrost, N. Wiebe and S. Lloyd (2017), Quantum machine learning, Nature, Vol. 549, pp. 195-202​​​​​​.
  4. D. Dong and I. R. Petersen (2010), Quantum control theory and applications: A survey, IET Control Theory & Applications, Vol. 4, pp. 2651-2671​​​​.
